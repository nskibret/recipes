Ingredients:
	-1 teaspoon butter
	-1 teaspoon olive oil
	-3/4 cup nonfat greek yogurt
	-1/2 teaspoon pepper powder
	-2 medium size eggs
	
Directions
	-boil the eggs
	-mix the butter and olive oil and microwave it for 33 seconds
	-add pepper powder and mix it well 
	-add the greek yogurt
	-peel the eggs
	-add the eggs and smash it properly 
	-enjoy easy breakfast
